We Are Creative Folks

What would be a better way to propagate entrepreneurial attitude than entrepreneurs, who inspire students with their stories. Students get important first hand knowledge and hear about diverse experiences. We build connections between students and business world, whether by chatting over a pint or by panel discussions. With these connections both students and entrepreneurs get tools and contacts for the future.
